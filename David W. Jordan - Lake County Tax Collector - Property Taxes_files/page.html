This morning our service centers held a Honorary Flag Lowering Ceremony to honor those who gave their life in service to our great country. This event occurs promptly at sunrise bringing the flag to half-staff, then raising it to full height at noon until sunset on Memorial Day.

Honorary Guests:
Tavares- Keith Truenow , U.S. Airforce, with his son Ashton
Clermont- Stanley Lay, U.S. Army and George Williams, U.S. Army...
Leesburg- Todd Smith, U.S. Army and Claude Hucorne, Vietnam veteran.

Thank you to all for your service and sacrifice!
